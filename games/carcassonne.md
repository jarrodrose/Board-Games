## Misc Notes
### Carcassonne Big Box 6 (2017)
Containing:
- Base Game w/ River and Abbot expansions
- Inns & Cathedrals
- Traders & Builders
- The Flying Machines
- The Messengers
- The Ferries
- The Goldmines
- Mage & Witch
- The Robbers
- Crop Circles II

This was released in 2017 with the cyan box, and then in 2021 in the beige box. Chief differences are reported to be, the beige box has a road at the river start, and a monastery by the river end. And the cities are more crowded.
